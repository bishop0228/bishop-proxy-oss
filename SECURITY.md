# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in bishop-proxy,
please report it privately to:

**security@mybishop.ai**

Please include:

- A description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- The affected file(s) and, if known, the affected commit
- Your assessment of impact

We will acknowledge receipt within 5 business days and aim to provide an
initial assessment within 10 business days.

Please do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until we have had a
reasonable opportunity to investigate and respond.

## Scope

In scope:

- The bishop-proxy source code in this repository
- The deployed Worker at `proxy.mybishop.ai` (operated by Bishop AI LLC)

Out of scope:

- Upstream dependencies (report to the upstream project)
- Physical access attacks
- Social engineering
- Issues in client software that consumes bishop-proxy

## Safe Harbor

Security research conducted in good faith and in accordance with this
policy is authorized under the Security Audit Carveout in `LICENSE.md`.
We will not pursue legal action against researchers who:

- Make a good-faith effort to avoid privacy violations, data destruction,
  and service interruption
- Report vulnerabilities promptly through the channel above
- Do not publicly disclose vulnerabilities before we have had a
  reasonable opportunity to respond
